### **1. Google Business Profile** ⭐ **MOST IMPORTANT**

This gets you on Google Maps and knowledge panel (like those other businesses you saw).

**Steps:**

1. Go to: **https://business.google.com**
2. Click "Create a Business Profile" or "Manage now"
3. Sign in with your Gmail
4. Fill in details:

   ```
   Business Name: Hassan Bookshop by Horumar
   Category: Book Store
   Also select: Stationery Store, School Supply Store

   Address: Global Apartments, Section One
            Eastleigh, Nairobi, Kenya

   Phone: 0722 979547
   Website: https://hassan.horumarin.com

   Hours: Monday-Sunday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
   (Adjust to your actual hours)
   ```

5. **Verify Your Business:**

   - Google will send a postcard with verification code
   - Takes 5-7 days to arrive
   - Enter the code when it arrives

6. **After Verification:**

   - Add 10-20 photos of your store
   - Add photos of products
   - Add logo/cover photo
   - Write business description (use our optimized one)
   - Enable messaging
   - Post updates weekly

7. **Get Reviews:**
   - Ask satisfied customers to leave Google reviews
   - Share this link with them after verification:
     `https://g.page/r/YOUR_BUSINESS_ID/review`
   - Aim for 20+ reviews (5 stars each)
   - Respond to all reviews

**Impact:** This alone can get you in top 3 for local searches! 📍

### **2. Submit to Google Search Console** 🔍

Tells Google your site exists and to index it.

**Steps:**

1. Go to: **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Click "Start now" and sign in
3. Add property: `https://hassan.horumarin.com`
4. Verify ownership (choose HTML tag method)
5. Submit sitemap: `https://hassan.horumarin.com/sitemap.xml`
6. Click "Request Indexing" for your homepage

**Check Weekly:**

- See what keywords you rank for
- Fix any crawl errors
- Monitor click-through rates
